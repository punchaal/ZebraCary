A Bit about Zebra

It is hard to believe that we started this rewarding adventure only three years ago. We are so proud and satisfied when parents tell us how much their child’s confidence and performance has improved at school since they started with us. The success of our program is also shown by the fact that our students have won awards at provincial and national levels in competitive events such as FLL and WRO. Our students have also been selected to represent Canada in the Robotics Olympiad.
